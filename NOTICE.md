## Re-analyzed datasets

This project is a re-analysis of the public **ProteomeTools** synthetic-peptide
datasets. Please cite the original publications and data depositions:

- Zolg et al., *Nature Methods*, 2017 — PRIDE **PXD004732**.
- Gessulat et al. (Prosit), *Nature Methods*, 2019 — PRIDE **PXD010595**.
- Wilhelm et al., *Nature Communications*, 2021 — PRIDE **PXD021013**.
- Published spectral libraries: <https://www.proteometools.org/index.php?id=53>.
